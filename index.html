About M&S Secure Tech

Who We Are

M&S Secure Tech is a proudly South African AI-enabled security company operating a centralized, state-of-the-art control room providing 24/7 monitoring services to commercial, industrial, and residential clients.

Our core value proposition combines real-time video surveillance across distributed camera networks with AI-driven event detection and analytics for proactive threat identification — positioning us at the intersection of traditional security services and modern technology.

We deliver coordinated armed response dispatch and incident management through professional control room operations with specialized staff roles and enterprise-grade infrastructure with built-in scalability.
